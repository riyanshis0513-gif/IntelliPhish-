About PhishGuard

PhishGuard is an AI-powered cybersecurity platform designed to detect phishing attacks, malicious URLs, and suspicious emails in real-time. Our mission is to make the internet safer for everyone.

🎯 Our Mission

To protect users from cyber threats by providing intelligent, fast, and reliable phishing detection tools. We aim to reduce online fraud and increase digital awareness.

🚀 Our Vision

To become a globally trusted cybersecurity platform that empowers individuals and organizations to browse safely without fear of phishing attacks.

📧 Email Analysis

Identify phishing emails and suspicious content before it causes harm.

📊 Threat Reports
